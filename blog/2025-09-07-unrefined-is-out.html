Unrefined is out

September 7, 2025

My book Unrefined came out a couple of days ago. If you ordered a copy, you probably have it already.

If you haven't ordered it yet, you can get it from the University of Chicago Press website, bookshop.org, Amazon, your local bookstore, or ask your library to order a copy.
